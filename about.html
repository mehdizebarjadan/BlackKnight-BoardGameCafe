The Black Knight is a board game cafe located in Torotno, ON. The cafe offers coffee, snacks, and a massive board game library. Customers will enjoy a comfortable space, with knowledgeable staff and a relaxed atmosphere. An evening at The Black Knight is hassle-free entertainment and a chance to spend time face to face with family and friends.

The Board Game Community in Toronto is a vibrant growing thing. There are dozens of board game groups in the Toronto area. Groups of people meet and play games in less than perfect environments: cramped restaurants, college classrooms, and the back rooms of comic book shops. The Black Knight will provide a common space to enrich the board game playing experience and foster this growing community. The Black Knight will be your living room away from home.
