Gift Card

Sending a electronic gift voucher (e-voucher) is quick and easy. Vouchers can be used to pay all or part of a bill they're valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. The recipient will receive an email almost immediately and after they follow the quick step to activate, their e-voucher will be emailed to them straightaway. So they're perfect for a last minute gift! The voucher can be printed off and presented at the restaurant, or shown on a mobile phone as means of payment.
