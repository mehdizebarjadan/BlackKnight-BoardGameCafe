Working at Black Knight

We are always searching for passionate staff at Black Knight. If you love board games, craft beer, or great coffee, and the idea of working in a community excites you, we want to hear from you!

If you don't hear from us right away, it doesn't mean we don't think you're amazing! Our staffing needs are always changing, and we'll keep your information on hand for when opportunity knocks.

Please see positions available:

Manager

Ensures that the cafe operates efficiently and profitably while maintaining our commitment to quality. Some of the tasks involved are: coordinating the entire operation of Black Knight during scheduled shifts, responding to customer complaints, recruiting, training and motivating staff, helping in any area of the cafe when circumstances dictate among other duties.

Kitchen Staff

The Kitchen staff primary objective is to prepare, cook and present quickly and efficiently, with the key aim of delivering food to our customers within 10 minutes.

Other duties may include:

  • Assist in keeping the kitchen clean, hygienic and tidy, at all times
  • Always adhere to all company policies and procedures
  • Be involved and contribute at team meetings
